14 May 2013 Five years of Unity expertise looking for contracts

On the 28th of June, after four great years at Unity Technologies, I part ways with my friends to go explore for a while – doing consulting / contract work.

I have had a great time and seen absolutely crazy growth and things while at Unity. I definitely am leaving with a nice big bag of great memories. However I feel the need to stretch my legs a bit, so off I go.

So I am available for contracts from July 1st, offering up among other things, my five years of Unity expertise. For more details on the things that I do there is this site and my LinkedIn profile.

And, well, that’s pretty much it. Want to play? Please use the handy contact form I set up: Handy contact form.
